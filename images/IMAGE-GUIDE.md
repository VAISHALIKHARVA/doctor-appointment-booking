# MediBook – Images to download

Save these 3 images in this folder (`images/`) with the exact filenames below.  
Format: PNG or JPG. Recommended size: 800×800 px or similar square/large for quality.

---

## 1. `hero.png` (Hero section – top of home page)

**Description:**  
A friendly illustration or photo for “medical care” or “booking appointments”: e.g. a doctor with a patient, a person booking on a phone/laptop, or a simple hospital/clinic icon. Clean, professional, and positive. Avoid busy or dark images so it fits the light blue circle on the right of the hero.

**Used in:** The large circular area on the right of “Get medical care early.”

---

## 2. `how-it-works.png` (How to book section)

**Description:**  
An illustration or icon set for “steps” or “booking process”: e.g. a calendar with a checkmark, a short checklist, or 3 simple steps (choose → book → track). Light, minimal style so it fits inside the circular area. Can be a single combined image or one clear “booking steps” graphic.

**Used in:** The round circle next to the “Choose your doctor / Pick date & slot / Track your queue” list.

---

## 3. `feature.png` (Why MediBook section)

**Description:**  
An illustration or photo of a doctor or healthcare professional: e.g. a doctor with a stethoscope, a friendly clinician, or a simple “doctor/healthcare” character. Professional and approachable. Should look good inside a rounded white card on a light blue background.

**Used in:** The square card on the left of “Why MediBook?” (before the text and “Read More” button).

---

## Filenames (exact)

- `hero.png`  
- `how-it-works.png`  
- `feature.png`  

If you use `.jpg` instead, rename the file to e.g. `hero.jpg` and update the `src` in `index.html` from `images/hero.png` to `images/hero.jpg` (same for the other two).
